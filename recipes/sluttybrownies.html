The Ultimate Slutty Brownies

Go back to main page

Description

Slutty Brownies rose to internet fame when The Londoner came up with the viral name to describe a brownie recipe with layers of cookie dough and Oreo on the popular website Kevin and Amanda. While some Americans might take offense to the name, it comes down to a difference in English dialects as the word “slutty” refers to a dirty or messy appearance in UK English.

Ingredients

Oreo Layer

  • 1/2 cup salted butter softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar tightly packed
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Brownie Layer

  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons salted butter melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips melted
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Other

  • 20 Oreo Cookies divided
  • 1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a metal 9x9 pan with parchment paper.
  2. Make the cookie layer. In a large mixing bowl use a hand mixer to cream together softened butter with brown sugar and white sugar. Add in egg and vanilla extract and mix until combined. Add in baking soda, salt, and flour and mix until just combined. Use a rubber spatula or spoon to stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Press cookie dough into the bottom of the prepared pan. Arrange 16 Oreo cookies on top.
  4. Make the brownie layer. Pour melted butter into a large mixing bowl. Whisk in sugar by hand until smooth, 30 seconds. Add in eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk 1 minute. Whisk in melted chocolate until combined and smooth. Use a rubber spatula to stir in flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined.
  5. Gently pour on top of the Oreo layer in the prepared pan. Crush remaining Oreo cookies and sprinkle on top and spread remaining 1/2 cup of chocolate chips on top as well.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-55 minutes. Cool completely before removing from the pan and slicing.
